Careers

One thing that is deeply rooted in the club is that amFOSS is helping its fellow members get their foot in the door with the tech industry. Our members are hence able to land a seat in the top universities abroad, boosting their career ahead. We are exposed to various opportunities too, which give us a feel of what it's like to be working in the industry for real.

Did you know?

amFOSS is one of the oldest student community at Amrita University. This club has been active throughout 10 years. amFOSS is the only university club that follows a culture where juniors are mentored by seniors. Moreover, the university club provides better emphasis on molding the students in a better human being with strong and versatile technical skillset.

Why you should be here.

Do you have that drive to work hard to reach heights and thrive for good causes? Then this is the place you should be. Our training and guidance have landed our alumni in the best places to work all over the world. More than that we work as a team to bring about some difference in socially relevant issues.

What we do.

The solid training given by the club to its students is one of a kind. With one of the highest numbers of Google Summer of Codes from a university in India, our students excel in all aspects of Computer Science and Engineering. We give emphasis on what the industry really requires and instill the skills that the classrooms do not provide in students.

The Fun!

This is not a mere technical club. What we vision here is the wholesome development of students.Apart from the social services, We organize exciting activities like debates, hackathons, and sports sessions to improve your interpersonal skills, leadership qualities, presentation skills and of course your physical abilities. Yes! A sound mind needs a sound body. So Welcome aboard!
